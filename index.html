About Me

I'm Edmond Nathan, and I am a certified Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst. I possess a diverse skill set that encompasses problem-solving, data cleaning and wrangling, retrieving data from databases, data analysis, report and dashboard creation using Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and R. Throughout my experience, I have developed expertise in utilizing various tools, including R Programming, SQL, MS Excel, Power BI, Power Query, and Power Pivot. I find great satisfaction in using my problem-solving and analytical skills to fulfill clients' needs and bring smiles to their faces.

My approach combines technical skills with clear communication to make complex data accessible to stakeholders at all levels. I believe data tells a story, and my job is to translate that story into actionable strategies.
